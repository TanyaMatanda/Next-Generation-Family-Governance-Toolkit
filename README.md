## Introduction
The Next-Generation Decision GPT is a powerful tool designed to assist family-business successors in making informed, evidence-based choices about their roles within their enterprises. 

## Purpose
This toolkit aims to provide resources and insights that empower successors, ensuring they can navigate the complexities of family business dynamics with confidence and clarity.

## Features
- **Informed Decision-Making**: Leverage data and insights to support your decision-making process.
- **Role Clarity**: Understand the various responsibilities and expectations associated with family business roles.
- **Evidence-Based Choices**: Utilize established frameworks and research to guide your decisions.
